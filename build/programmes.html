Our Programs

From 3 months to 5 years old, the level of care we offer is unparalleled

Infants

3 to 18 months

We know how difficult it can be for your baby to be away from you for the first time — and vice versa! Our 1:3 ratio means educators can focus on each child, ensuring the transition is as smooth as possible, for both parents and infants.

Your little one's day-to-day will include Baby Yoga, ample outdoor time, lots of cuddles, and plenty of play that encourages exploration, skill development, and the opportunity to move freely.

Toddlers

1½ to 2½ years old

Your baby is growing up! And our educators are here to help them navigate that exciting time between babyhood and childhood. Our 1:5 ratio means educators are hands-on when it comes to nap time, potty training and more.

At this age, we incorporate some simple meditation — great for self-regulating — into each day. Plus, your little one will be engaged in a variety of age-appropriate physical activities, arts & crafts, and free play. They'll just love the many opportunities to get outside, get messy and have FUN!

Pre-Schoolers

2½ to 5 years old

Our educators will help prepare your child for their next big milestone: school!

During their time with us, your preschooler will acquire the necessary social and emotional skills to succeed in a 'big kid' classroom. TLC's very own early literacy program provides preschoolers with the foundation they need to read at their own pace.

With a 1:8 ratio, our older group safely enjoys both indoor and outdoor free play, community walks, dance, gymnastics, non-competitive games and sports, puzzles, and even some simple math.

Follow us... We're social!
